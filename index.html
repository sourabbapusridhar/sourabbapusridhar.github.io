Biography

I am a second-year master’s student in the Systems, Control and Mechatronics program at the Chalmers University of Technology, Sweden. During my first-year, I was a part of the Chalmers Formula Student Driverless (CFSD) team, a student-driven project team building a high-performance autonomous racecar. During my time at CFSD20, I was responsible for Vision, Localization and Embedded Software.

Before Chalmers, I worked at Robert Bosch GmbH as a Senior Software Engineer, where I developed device drivers for safety-critical supply modules in all Bosch electrical braking system products. My reasearch interests include distributed Deep Learning, Autonomous Decision Making, Computer Vision, Robotics and Sensor Fusion.
